# 数据科学与大数据技术专业第二学位本科人才培养方案  
# Second Bachelor’s Degree Program in Data Science and Big Data Technology

---

## 一、培养目标 | I. Training Objectives

以计算机科学与技术、统计学等理论为基础，掌握大数据建模与分析方法，具备数据采集、清洗、存储、分析与挖掘、大数据系统开发等技能。  
Based on computer science and statistics theories, students will master big data modeling and analysis methods and develop skills in collection, cleaning, storage, analysis, mining, and system development.

毕业生可在IT企业、金融机构、政府部门和科研机构从事相关工作或继续深造。  
Graduates may work in IT companies, finance, government, and research institutions or pursue further study.
